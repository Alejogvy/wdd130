Detailed Description

We will meet at our agency at 9 am where you will be provided with the necessary equipment for your rafting tour. Once everyone is equipped, we will begin our private car tour to the river entrance. Along the way, you can see different waterfalls Agoyan, Manto de la Novia and Pailón del Diablo that are located along the route. When we arrive at the river, you will be given a safety briefing so you know what to do on the raft, such as paddling orders and rescue techniques to keep the group safe on the river. We will first practice the commands on land before reaching the river. Once you have the necessary knowledge, then it is time for your rafting experience through a beautiful landscape. The tour takes approximately one hour 20 minutes on the river. At the end of the rafting section the car with the driver will be waiting for us. Then to finish the trip we will have a well-deserved lunch in Río Negro before returning to Baños, where it ends around 2pm.
